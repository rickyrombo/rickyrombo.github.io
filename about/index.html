I’m RickyRombo

I'm RickyRombo. I make music sometimes.

I like music that tells stories. I like music that paints a landscape. I like music that is just music and has no secret meaning, but only if it's good.

I have a lot of opinions and most of them are wrong. Despite being aware of this, I'll still fight you. Later I'll feel stupid, but it takes a while.

Hope you can enjoy some of the stuff I make.
